Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

The classic grilled cheese sandwich. Anyone can make it with only a few simple ingredients!

Ingredients

  • White bread, although any is fine
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the bread slices on one side.
  2. On a hot skillet, place one bread slice butter-side-down.
  3. Top with cheese.
  4. Place another slice of bread above the cheese, butter-side up.
  5. When the bottom slice is browned, flip the sandwich.
  6. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
