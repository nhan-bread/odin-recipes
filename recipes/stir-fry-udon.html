Stir-Fry Udon

Description

A simple noodle dish incorporating udon noodles with any meat and vegetables of your choice, perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 packs of udon
  • 1 scallion, with white and green parts separated
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 250 g pork or any meat of choice
  • 250 g cabbage or any vegetable of choice
  • Sauce
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1.5 tbsp dark soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Cut up cabbage and pork into long strips.
  2. Mince garlic and scallion.
  3. Boil udon for according to package instructions and quickly transfer to ice bath.
  4. Prepare sauce and mix well.
  5. In a heated pan, add oil and the white part of the scallion.
  6. Add pork and cook until browned.
  7. Add cabbage and cook until softened.
  8. Add udon noodles and stir.
  9. Add sauce and stir until the color is evenly spread.
  10. Garnish with green onions before serving.
